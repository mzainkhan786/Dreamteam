Our mission

At DreamTeam, we’re on a mission to unleash all that encumbered innovation pent up in tech organizations. We want to help you tap into your team so your people do work that matters and avoid work that doesn’t.

In fact, one of our core beliefs is that return on effort trumps return on investment when measuring a team’s performance.

DreamTeam

While leading engineering teams at world class companies like Snap and Amazon, I realized the information flow inside an organization is often broken. This causes a lack of alignment, which results in wasted effort.

DreamTeam was born out of the observation that despite how critical execution is to the success of an organization, there is no playbook for effective execution. We are on a mission to change that -- helping companies stay fast and lean at any scale.

Brent Barkman, Founder
